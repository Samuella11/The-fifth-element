Mizithra

A unique cheese with a devoted following, Mizithra has been a part of our family recipes from the very beginning.

But our most famous dish almost never made it on the menu. Our Spaghetti with Mizithra Cheese and Browned Butter is an old Dussin family recipe. When Guss Dussin applied for the first Old Spaghetti Factory’s business and liquor license, he was told he needed one more item on the menu. So, Guss suggested to his family the beloved Mizithra cheese recipe. And to this day, it’s the most popular dish on our menu.

Once you try it, you know.
